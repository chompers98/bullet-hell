The following two VGA-to-HDMI adapters were tested and the two projects (vga_demo and vga_moving_block posted onBrightspaceunder Assignments => EE354L Lab Manual Spring 2021) worked on a HDMI Monitor and also on a TV with HDMI input.
The Nexys-4 board itself, as part of its power-on self-test, displays an elaborate Nexys-4 advertisement screen through its VGA output port.
This worked on the HDMI Monitor but not on the TV. Maybe it has to do with its resolution requirement.

Adapters bought on amazon prime and tested:
